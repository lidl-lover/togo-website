togo is western afircan nation who is bordered by Ghana, Benin and Burkina faso and Ghana is Togos longest land border of 677 kilometers or 545 miles.

togo has 6 geographic regions there is the the coastal region with its low lying sandy beachesand holds the largest lake called the lake togo, next is the Ouatchi Plateau, which stretches about 20 miles (32 km) inland at an elevation of some 200 to 300 feet (60 to 90 metres), after the ouatchi plateau we have the tableland which can reach an elevations of 1300 to 1500 feet(400 to 460 metres), next is the togo mountains also known as Togo-Atakora Mountains, which run across central Togo from the south-southwest to the north-northeast. Part of a chain that begins in the Akwapim Hills of Ghana (see Akwapim-Togo ranges), the range ends in Benin, where it is known as the Atakora Mountains. Mount Agou (Baumann Peak), which rises to about 3,235 feet (986 metres), is the highest mountain in Togo and fianally the last region in togo is the Oti river which is a savanna region drained by the Oti river.
